Bình Dương

Bình Dương is often known as a province with a development of technology, factories and large businesses.

Topography


Bình Dương is in the Southeast region of Vie Nam. Surrounding Bình Dương are major provinces and cities. Binh Duong is a province in the Southeast region of Vietnam, bordering Binh Phuoc to the North, Tay Ninh to the West, Dong Nai to the East and Ho Chi Minh City to the South.


Weather


The weather here in Binh Duong is same like the weather in the Southeast region, includes 2 main seasons: dry season and rain season. Rain season often start from May to the end of October. In first months here often appear short and big rains. From July, it starts to rain regularly.


In dry season, In the dry season, the average annual humidity ranges from 76–80%, with a peak of 86% in September and a low of 66% in February.The average annual temperature in Binh Duong is 26–27 °C (79–81 °F), with the highest recorded at 39.3 °C (102.7 °F)


Agriculture and Industry


Binh Duong has bazan that is convenient for rubber and coffee trees; alluvial deposits region with many paddy and fruit gardens. The network of rivers and canals are complete with Dong Nai, Sai Gon, Be rivers. Situated in a convenient center, Binh Duong will be the door for international connectivity, trades, services and exchanges, includes the best science technology.
