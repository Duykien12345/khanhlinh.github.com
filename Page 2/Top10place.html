Top 10 places you should visit when you come to Binh Duong


1. Phu An Bamboo Village

Located in Ben Cat District, Binh Duong province, Phu An Bamboo Village (also known as Phu An Ecological Museum and Botanical Museum), is one of the biggest bamboo Museum in Southeast Asia. This multipurpose center do scientific research and ecosystem conservation while promoting the awareness of the environment and the value of ecosystem tourism in local community

2. Hoi Khanh Pagoda

Hoi Khanh Pagoda is a historic Buddhist temple built in Binh Duong in 1741. In 2013, the temple received a certificate recognizing the statue of Buddha entering Nirvana on the roof as the longest historical monument in Asia. This record has been recognised by Asia Record Book, which is located in India.

3. New City Park

With an acreage of 70 hectaries, New City Park is an ideal place for tourists to relax with a beautiful landscapes, lakes and entertainment center for children.

4. Phu Cuong Cathedral

Phu Coung Cathedral is situated on 104 Lac Long Quan street, Phu Cuong Ward, Thu Dau Mot City. This is the biggest cathedral in Binh Duong. It is built in Gothic style.

5. Tay Tang Pagoda

Far from Ho Chi Minh City 30 kilometers, it is loacted on 46B Thich Quang Duc street, Chanh Nghia, Thu Dau Mot City. Tay Tang Pagoda has a unique architecture style of Tay Tang people and are different with other temples in Vie Nam. Tay Tang Pagoda has a biggest Buddhist statue made by hair in Vie Nam and also the only one in the world.

6. Dai Nam tourism

Dai Nam tourism is in the south of Binh Duong, which is an ideal place for holiday because of great architecture and good entertainment services.


The construction of the biggest tourism site strated from 1999, far from Ho Chi Minh City 40 kilometers. This tourism site includes Dai Nam Van Hien temple, an entertainment center, a zoo, shopping center, hotels and food center. The park is home of white lion, rhino, monkeys, tiger, elephants and other animals, many of them are rare in Vie Nam.


Dai Nam Sea is 21.6 hectares wide, with a total water surface area of 20,000 square meters, the coastline is 1.4 kilometers long. The artificial sea has a beach with artificial waves up to 1.6m high, which is the largest wave pool in Vietnam by Murphys Waves Ltd. Famous Scottish design and manufacture.

7. Wind and Water Cafe

Wind and Water Cafe located on 6/28T zone 3, Thu Dau Mot City. The Cafe has received many international design awards, one of that is the International Architecture Award (IAA) in 2008. The Wind and Water Cafe is designed and constructed by Vo Trong Nghia architect, the main material is bamboo, one popular kind of plants in Binh Duong.

8. Phu Long Temple

Phu Long Temple is an earliest temple built in Binh Duong. This place is recognised as Historic Relics- Country Level Architecture in 28/12/2001.

9. Thuy Chau Tourist Site

Thuy Chau is an artificial ecosystem tourist site with the total acreage of more than 18 hectaries. This tourist site has many lakes, fontains and trees for people to immerse themselves in natural world and to relax after a day working.

10. Mira Hotel

The Mira is the first 5-star hotel in Binh Duong designed with 12 floors with 164 rooms, 22 luxury apartments with luxurious furniture.
