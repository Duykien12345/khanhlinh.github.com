Bình Dương


Bình Dương is often known by people as the province of convergence and the development. But there are many interesting things about in Bình Dương. Let's explore with us!


Pictures


Development


Binh Duong are developing. There are many businesses and factories producing goods and trade with another cities. Also, there is a technological advancement with a range of technology devices and engineers working in technology field.

People

Most people in Binh Duong comes from other cities in Vie Nam. Local people here are really hospitable. Thanks to the hospitality and friendliness of people and the technological advancement, this place has attract a lot of tourists in many countries working and studying in Binh Duong.

Culture

Binh Duong has many traditional crafts, such as wood carving, pottery making and lacquer paintings. In the past, Binh Duong's art ceramics, lacquers and sculptures have participated in international fairs and have also been exported to France and many other countries.
