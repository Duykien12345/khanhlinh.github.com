Top 10 traditional food in Binh Duong you should eat once

1. Banh Beo Bi

Banh Beo Bi is round pieces with green beans stuff in the middle, served with Bi, which is a mixture of sliced pork and ram skin. Then, add some spice, salad with sweet and sour sauce is able to enjoy. This food has been made for 100 years.

2. Banh Khot

Banh Khot is also one of the favorite food in Binh Duong. Banh Khot are yellow round pieces with a shrimp in the middle of each. People often eat this food with salad and a mixture of fish sauce and chili.

3. Goi Ga MangCut (Mangosteen Chicken Salad)

Ripened mangosteen are chosen for the crispiness, with a taste of sweet and sour. The chicken are boiled to become tough and firm pieces of meat. This two main ingredients are mixed with sweet and sour chili fish sauce and coriander.

4. Nem Lai Thieu

Nem chua is made by local people by the traditional recipe, from choosing carefully fresh pork thighs to delicious seasoning. It taste sweet and sour and a little bit spicy.

5. Ga Bo Xoi (Chicken wrapped with Sticky Rice)

The chicken is marinated with spices, spread with honey outside. Usually, the chicken is ate with puffed sticky rice.

6. Goi Ngo Luc Binh Tron Tom Thit (Water hyacinth salad mixed with shrimp and meat)

The water hyacinth salad that is sliced into thin pieces, soaked with alum and lemon juice, shrimp and boiled pork belly cut into thin pieces and then mixed with laksa leaves, lemon, sugar, chili, fish sauce with a little roasted peanuts on top.Water hyacinth salad with shrimp and meat dipped in sweet and sour fish sauce served with fried puff pastry.

7. Bo Nuong Ngoi (Grilled Beef with tile)

Beef is spread on tiles on fire to keep the softness and natural flavor of the meat.The beef that was just placed on was charred and fragrant, grilled with sliced bananas and sour star fruit. Roll a piece of soft sweet beef with rice paper with perilla leaves and cucumber, add bananas and star fruit for a bit of acrid flavor and then dip into the bowl of fish sauce.

8. Lau Bo Nhung Mam Ruoc (Beef hotpot dipped with mam ruoc)

Mam ruoc is seasoned directly into the broth, in addition to pork belly, onions and spices such as lemongrass, chili, noodles and sugar.The hotpot has the fragnant of lemongrass, pork belly and the taste of mam ruoc. Dip each piece of beef into a fragrant, bubbling hotpot and serve with fresh vermicelli.

9. Bun Tom (Shrimp Rice Noodles)

The rice noodles is small and flexible. The broth taste sweet, not forget the fresh and sweet shrimps.

10. Banh Xeo Tom Nhay (Jumping Shrimp Pancake)

As its name, the fresh shrimps still jump when putting them on hot oil. The yellow pancakes are lined with red shrimp, topped with a few sprouts, chopped onions and scallions. Roll a piece of pancake with some salad and then dip it in fish sauce, eat it while it's crispy, soft, sour and sweet.
